01 · Overview

Executive Summary

A high-level summary of the proposed solution and its core value proposition for your financing business.

This proposal outlines the design, feasibility, and development plan for an EMI Recovery Software System — a robust, multi-platform solution that enables mobile device retailers to finance phones on EMI while maintaining complete control over device operability tied to payment compliance.

The system links every financed device to a centralized cloud platform from the moment of sale. If a customer defaults on their EMI cycle, the system automatically triggers a remote lock, rendering the device partially or fully inoperable — surviving factory resets, app uninstallation attempts, and other bypass methods through deep OS-level integration. Unlock is automatic upon confirmed payment or manual via the shop admin panel.

The platform serves three distinct user groups: shop owners/admins (device management & EMI tracking), customers (payment portal & EMI status), and the devices themselves (lock enforcement agent). It covers Android, iOS, and Android TV with technically appropriate approaches for each platform's constraints.

02 · Scope

Platform Coverage & Approach

Each platform requires a distinct technical strategy due to fundamental differences in OS architecture, permissions, and device management APIs.

🤖

Android

Smartphones & tablets (v8.0+)

  • Device Policy Controller (DPC) / Device Admin API for deep control
  • Device Owner mode — full-lock survives factory reset via DPC re-enrollment
  • KIOSK / LockTask mode to restrict device to a single lock screen
  • Work Profile & MDM integration (Android Enterprise)
  • Push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)
  • Background service with battery-ignore permission for persistent monitoring
  • Anti-uninstall protection via Device Admin privilege requirement
High Feasibility Best Control
🍎

iOS

iPhones & iPads (iOS 14+)

  • Apple Business Manager (ABM) + MDM enrollment at point of sale
  • Supervised Mode via Apple Configurator 2 or ABM DEP enrollment
  • MDM profile-based restrictions — disables apps, features, calling
  • Single App Mode to lock device to a payment/lock screen
  • APNs (Apple Push Notification Service) for remote commands
  • Remote wipe & lock via MDM protocol
  • Activation Lock tied to shop's Apple ID — requires shop credentials to unlock
Moderate Feasibility MDM Required
📺

Android TV

Smart TVs & TV boxes (v9.0+)

  • Custom Launcher replacement as default home app
  • Device Owner mode — same as Android smartphone approach
  • Lock screen overlay blocks all content until payment
  • Remote command delivery via cloud + FCM
  • Display a payment QR code / instructions on the lock screen
  • HDMI-CEC control optionally used to force screen on during lock state
  • Anti-bypass via Device Admin — cannot be uninstalled without admin revoke
High Feasibility Strong Control

Key iOS Caveat: Unlike Android, iOS does not allow third-party apps to "own" a device at the OS level without Apple Business Manager enrollment. This means iOS devices must be enrolled in your MDM profile at the point of sale — before they are handed to the customer. This is a one-time setup step per device and is the standard approach used by enterprise device management worldwide.

03 · Features

Core System Features

The system is divided into four functional modules that work together to provide end-to-end financing control.

Module A — Device Lock & Control Engine

🔐

Automated Lock Triggering

The system continuously monitors each device's EMI schedule. When a payment deadline passes (configurable grace period), the lock is automatically initiated via a remote command.

  • Configurable grace period (e.g., 3 days after due date)
  • Progressive lock: restrict apps first, then full lock
  • Emergency call remains available during lock state
  • Battery & SOS functions preserved at all times
🛡️

Factory Reset Survival

The most critical security requirement. Different mechanisms are used per platform to ensure the lock re-engages even after a factory reset.

  • Android: Device Owner DPC re-enrollment on first boot
  • Android TV: Same DPC approach via QR provisioning
  • iOS: ABM DEP enrollment — device checks in to MDM automatically on restore
  • Persistent enrollment survives all user-level resets
📲

On-Device Lock Screen

A custom branded lock screen is displayed when a device is locked, guiding the customer to make payment rather than leaving them confused.

  • Shows EMI amount due, due date, and contact info
  • QR code for quick payment link
  • Shop branding and logo
  • Option to call shop directly from lock screen
🔓

Automatic & Manual Unlock

Two pathways to unlock: the system automatically verifies payment via payment gateway webhook and sends an unlock command, or the admin can manually unlock from the dashboard.

  • Webhook-based auto-unlock on payment confirmation
  • Unlock propagated via FCM / APNs in <30 seconds
  • Admin manual unlock from web panel or mobile app
  • Full audit log of all lock/unlock events with timestamps

Module B — Admin Panel (Shop Owner Dashboard)

📊

Customer & Device Management

A comprehensive web-based admin dashboard for shop owners to manage their entire financed device portfolio.

  • Customer profiles with KYC documents, photos, contact
  • Device registry: IMEI, model, purchase date, plan details
  • EMI schedule creation and management per device
  • Bulk device status view (active/locked/overdue)
💰

EMI Tracking & Payments

Full visibility into payment history and upcoming dues with integrated payment collection tools.

  • EMI calendar view per customer and device
  • Overdue alerts with aging (7/15/30 days)
  • Manual payment recording for cash payments
  • Payment receipts auto-generated and emailed/SMS'd

Module C — Customer Mobile App

👤

Customer-Facing Interface

A lightweight app or web portal for customers to view their EMI status, receive reminders, and make payments directly.

  • EMI schedule and payment history
  • Upcoming EMI reminder notifications
  • Online payment via UPI, card, net banking
  • Download payment receipts
🔔

Reminder & Notification System

Multi-channel reminders reduce defaults proactively before the lock is ever needed.

  • Push notifications 7 days, 3 days, 1 day before due
  • SMS reminders via gateway (Twilio / Indian SMS providers)
  • WhatsApp Business API messages
  • In-app message thread with shop

Module D — Cloud Backend & APIs

☁️

Centralized Backend Platform

A scalable, secure cloud backend that coordinates all device commands, payment events, and user data.

  • REST API for mobile apps and admin panel
  • Real-time device status updates via WebSocket
  • Scheduled job engine for EMI cycle processing
  • Multi-tenant architecture for multiple shop locations
💳

Payment Gateway Integration

Seamless integration with popular payment providers for automated payment verification and unlock triggering.

  • Razorpay / PayU / Cashfree (India market)
  • UPI, cards, net banking, wallets supported
  • Webhook-based instant payment confirmation
  • Reconciliation reports and export to Tally/Excel

05 · Security

Security Architecture

Security is the backbone of this system. Multiple layers of protection ensure devices remain locked even under aggressive bypass attempts.

Threat / Bypass Attempt Platform Mitigation Mechanism Effectiveness
App Uninstallation Android / TV Device Admin privileges — system prompts admin deactivation before uninstall is allowed. Deactivation triggers immediate lock command. Very High
Factory Reset Android / TV Device Owner DPC enrollment is re-triggered on first boot via QR code / NFC provisioning baked into firmware. MDM profile re-downloads automatically. High
Factory Reset iOS Apple Business Manager DEP (Device Enrollment Program) — device phones home to Apple's servers on first boot and re-enrolls in shop's MDM automatically. Very High
Offline Mode / SIM Removal All Lock state is maintained locally on the device. Device stays locked even with no internet. Unlock requires a valid online token from our servers. High
Custom ROM / Rooting Android / TV Device integrity attestation via Google Play Integrity API (formerly SafetyNet). Rooted/modded devices are flagged and lock is preserved. No SafetyNet = no unlock. Moderate
ADB Debugging Attack Android / TV ADB can be disabled via Device Policy. Device Owner can enforce no-ADB policy across the whole device. High
Jailbreak iOS Jailbroken devices lose MDM supervision and trigger a tamper alert on the server. MDM commands are rejected on jailbroken devices by Apple's APIs. Moderate
Server API Attack Cloud JWT tokens with short expiry, device certificate pinning, rate limiting, encrypted communication (TLS 1.3), and server-side input validation throughout. Very High
06 · Feasibility

Feasibility Analysis

A structured assessment of technical, operational, legal, and financial feasibility to confirm this project is viable and well-scoped.

⚙️ Technical Feasibility

High
All core technical components — Android Device Policy Controller, Apple MDM enrollment, and cloud-based command delivery — are well-documented, production-proven APIs used by enterprise MDM vendors like VMware Workspace ONE, Microsoft Intune, and Jamf. This is not experimental technology. The only complexity lies in proper provisioning setup at point of sale, which is a one-time, configurable workflow.

⚖️ Legal & Regulatory Feasibility

Review Needed
Remote device locking for non-payment is legally practiced across the financing industry globally (carrier locks, EMI financing). The customer must be clearly informed and must consent in the financing agreement. A well-drafted Terms & Conditions document and explicit customer consent at enrollment covers this. We recommend a brief legal review for your jurisdiction (India-specific IT Act compliance and RBI guidelines on device financing).

🏪 Operational Feasibility

High
The enrollment process for each new device at point of sale takes approximately 5–10 minutes for Android and 10–15 minutes for iOS (ABM enrollment). This is operationally simple with proper staff training. The admin panel is designed for non-technical shop staff. Bulk enrollment tools will be provided for shops onboarding multiple devices at once. Remote management means no technical staff needed on-site post-setup.

📈 Scalability Feasibility

Very High
The cloud architecture is horizontally scalable. The backend is containerized (Docker + Kubernetes-ready) so it can handle from 10 devices to 100,000+ devices without architectural changes. Multi-tenant support allows the platform to serve multiple shop branches or to be white-labeled and sold as a SaaS product to other financing retailers — a potential business expansion opportunity.

🍎 iOS-Specific Feasibility Note

Conditional
iOS offers significantly less control than Android to third-party apps. Full lock enforcement on iOS is only possible through Apple's official MDM protocol with Supervised Mode, which requires Apple Business Manager enrollment. This is achievable but requires the shop to have an Apple Business Manager account (free, but requires a DUNS number or equivalent business registration). Without this, iOS locking is limited to app-level restrictions only.

💰 Financial Feasibility

Strong ROI
The cost of development is a one-time investment that significantly reduces financial risk from defaulting customers. Even a reduction of 5–10% in EMI defaults across a portfolio of 100+ financed devices can recover the development cost within the first quarter of operation. Additionally, the platform can be monetized as a SaaS product for other dealers, creating a recurring revenue stream.

08 · Risks

Risk Assessment

Identified risks and their mitigation strategies to ensure smooth development and operation.

Apple ABM Enrollment Delay

Apple Business Manager account setup requires business verification (DUNS number) which can take 2–4 weeks. This could delay iOS development.

✓ Mitigation: Begin ABM registration on Day 1. Android work proceeds in parallel with no dependency on Apple approval.

Medium

Google Play Integrity (Rooted Devices)

Determined users rooting their Android device could potentially bypass some restrictions if they use a custom ROM that spoofs device integrity checks.

✓ Mitigation: Device integrity is checked on every unlock attempt. Rooted devices are flagged and remain locked. Financing contracts should include a clause prohibiting rooting.

Medium

Scope Creep

Complex systems often grow beyond initial scope. Adding custom reporting, multiple payment gateways, or regional language support mid-development increases cost and timeline.

✓ Mitigation: Detailed scope document signed before development begins. Change requests handled via formal CR process with updated cost and timeline estimates.

Low

Legal Challenge by Customer

A customer whose device was locked could challenge the lock in consumer court if the financing agreement is ambiguous about device control rights.

✓ Mitigation: A lawyer-vetted financing agreement with explicit device control consent clause. Grace period before locking, and clear payment instructions shown during lock state.

Low

Push Notification Delivery Failure

FCM or APNs delivery is not guaranteed (OEM battery optimization, network issues) which could delay lock commands reaching a device.

✓ Mitigation: Lock state is also enforced locally on-device via a periodic timer check. Device checks in with the server regularly; if payment status is overdue, it self-locks without needing a push command.

Low
